Frequently Asked Questions

What is ApplySmart and who is it for?
ApplySmart is a toolkit powered by hiring expertise and smart AI prompts. It’s for anyone applying for jobs – whether you’re a school leaver, career changer, or seasoned professional.
How are the prompts created?
Every prompt is written by professionals with real recruitment experience and enhanced using AI. They're tested with hiring managers across sectors for clarity and impact.
Is ApplySmart better than ChatGPT alone?
Yes. While ChatGPT is powerful, ApplySmart tailors the experience using prompts that are job-specific and written by hiring experts. It removes the guesswork.
Can I request a custom prompt?
Absolutely. You can request tailored prompts for specific roles or industries via our Tailored or Premium packs. Just follow the link and submit your details.
Do I need any technical skills to use ApplySmart?
No technical knowledge is needed. Our prompts work in plain English and are designed for ease of use. Just copy, paste, and personalise.
